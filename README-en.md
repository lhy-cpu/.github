## 🧭 My Learning Journey & Tech Roadmap

From high-level abstractions ➡️ Bare-metal hardware ➡️ The frontier of AI, building full-lifecycle development capabilities.

<br>

### ☕ Phase 1: Foundation (Java)
📌 **Goal**: Establish solid Object-Oriented Programming principles.
- 💻 **Software Dev**: Cross-platform desktop apps & JavaWeb backends.
- 📱 **Mobile Dev**: Built native Android applications.
- 🎮 **Game Modding**: Scripted highly customizable Minecraft (Java Edition) plugins.

<div align="center">⏬</div>

### ⚙️ Phase 2: Bare Metal (C/C++ & Hardware)
📌 **Goal**: Understand "how computers actually work under the hood."
- 🔧 **Microcontrollers**: STC MCU, STM32, ESP Series, Infineon AURIX™ TC automotive chips.

- 🔮 **Frontier Tech**: Actively researching FPGA architectures & HDL.
